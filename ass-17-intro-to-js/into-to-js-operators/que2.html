Que2 : Describe the categorization of operators in JS . based on their functionality. provide examples of each category ?

Ans : There are five types of operators in JS (1) : Airthmetic operator (2) : Assignment operator (3) : Logical Operator (4) : Comparision operator (5) : Bitwise operator

  • (1) Airthmetic Operators -> Here we use +,-,*,/,++,--,%

  • (2) Assignment Operators -> Here we use +=,-=,*=,/=

  • (3) comparison Operators -> Here we use ==, <=,>=, !=

  • (4) logical Operators -> Here we use &&, ||, !

  • (5) Bitwise operator -> &,|,~(not),^(X-OR),<<(left shift),(right shift)
