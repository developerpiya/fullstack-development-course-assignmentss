Discuss the precedence and associativity of operators in js. Why is understanding these concept importat ?

Precedence Operator precedence determines how operators are parsed concerning each other. Operators with higher precedence become the operands of operators with lower precedence.

Associativity The associativity of an operator specifies the order in which operations of the same precedence are performed. Left-to-right associativity means that operations are performed from left to right
